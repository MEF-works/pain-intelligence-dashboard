## BREAKDOWN PATTERNS:
- **"Charged but never got..."** - Payment processing works but delivery/service fails
- **"No response"** - Customer service complete breakdown 
- **"Confusing"** - Billing, policies, processes that make no sense to customers
- **System vs Reality Gaps** - Digital systems showing success while real world shows failure
